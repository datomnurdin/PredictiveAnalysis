Principal Component Analysis (Clustering Analysis) based on KPI (Total Net Revenue)

Explaination: https://github.com/datomnurdin/PredictiveAnalysis/blob/master/InternalData/cluster.ipynb

Finding & Analysis: Group customers by how similar their total net revenue in each month are. By doing so we uncovered some interesting relationships and, most importantly, better understood the customer situation regarding this important revenue. We saw how most customers improved in the time lapse we considered, but we were also able to discover a group of customers with a high prevalence of the revenue that, far from improving their situation, are increasing the total net revenue.
